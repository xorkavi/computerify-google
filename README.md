# Copy-that

Turn your writing into on-brand copy for Computer -- instantly.

## What it does

Copy-that rewrites text in Google Docs and Google Slides to match Computer's brand voice. Select your text, click **Fix copy**, and it's done. The original text is replaced in-place with an on-brand version.

It handles everything from customer emails and sales decks to slide bullets and internal messages -- adapting the tone automatically based on what you're writing.

## How to use it

### In Google Docs

1. Highlight the text you want to fix
2. Go to **Extensions > Copy-that > Fix copy**
3. Or click **Fix copy** in the sidebar panel on the right

### In Google Slides

1. Click on a text box (or select multiple text boxes)
2. Go to **Extensions > Copy-that > Fix copy**
3. Or click **Fix copy** in the sidebar panel on the right

That's it. The text is replaced with the on-brand version.

## What it fixes

Copy-that applies Computer's brand and tone of voice guidelines, including:

- **Voice and tone** -- warm, conversational, human. Never corporate or robotic.
- **Jargon removal** -- replaces marketing-speak and tech-waffle with plain language.
- **Correct formatting** -- sentence case headings, en dashes, smart quotes, Oxford commas.
- **Brand rules** -- Computer as a proper noun, "it" not "he/she/they", "teammates" not "users", "Team Intelligence" not "artificial intelligence."
- **Context-aware tone** -- apologetic for outage emails, confident for sales decks, punchy for slide bullets, friendly for internal comms.

## Tips

- **Multi-select in Slides** -- select multiple text boxes and they'll all be fixed at once.
- **Paragraph styles are preserved** -- heading and body text formatting stays intact in Slides.
- **Works with any length** -- from a single sentence to full paragraphs.

## Need help?

If something isn't working, try closing and reopening the sidebar, or refreshing the document. If the issue persists, reach out to your admin.
